Frequently Asked Questions

What is Zyra?

Zyra is an online game shop offering a curated selection of digital games across various genres like action, horror, FPS, and RPG. We aim to provide a seamless and secure platform for gamers to explore, purchase, and enjoy their favorite titles.

Do I need to create an account to shop?

Yes, a Zyra account is required to make purchases. It allows you to securely store your purchase history, receive game updates, and re-download games when needed.

How do I purchase a game from Zyra?

Browse the catalog and click on a game to view details.
Click “Add to Cart” to include it in your shopping basket (Make sure you're logged in).
Go to the checkout page and complete the payment.
Once your payment is processed, you will receive an email with either a download link or an activation key (depending on the game).
